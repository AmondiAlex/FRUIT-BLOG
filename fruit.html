Fruit Salad with Strawberry Syrup and Juices

Fruit Salad is a healthy and better way of considering variety of fruits in one serving. Its the best choice ever.

    Ingredients

  • Pomegranate
  • Watermelon
  • Kiwi
  • Blueberries
  • Pineapples
  • Strawberry Syrup

    Preparation

  1. Wash the fruits thoroughly
  2. Chop,Slice,Peel,Cut and Grate where necessary
  3. Mix the fruits and add the strawberry syrup
  4. Serve in a pretty plate
